# City Rules

These are the official SouthSide RP city rules that all players must follow.

### 1. Common Sense

Treat others with respect. Don’t harass, grief, or exploit.

### 2. No Fail RP

Always stay in character. Use /OOC sparingly and only for real issues.

### 3. Powergaming & Metagaming

- **Powergaming:** Forcing actions or outcomes on others.
- **Metagaming:** Using out-of-character info for in-character advantage.

### 4. Value of Life

Value your character’s life realistically in every situation.

### 5. Combat Logging

Never disconnect or respawn to escape a situation.

Breaking rules can result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans depending on severity.
