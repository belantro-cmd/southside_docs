# Gang Overview

Gangs in SouthSide RP are story-driven factions with hierarchy and reputation systems.

### Key Points

- All gangs must be **approved** by staff before operating.
- Gang RP should focus on story, not constant violence.
- Each gang may claim limited **territory** in the city.

### Gang Types

- Street Gangs (Ballas, Families, Vagos, etc.)
- Organized Crime (Mafia, Cartel)
- MCs and Specialty Groups (Lost MC, Triads)
